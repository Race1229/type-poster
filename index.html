Cormorant Garamond

Christian Thalmann

Cormorant is a font family developed by designer Christian Thalmann and is highly influenced by Claude Garamont's immortal legacy. The project comprises of 9 different visual styles which consist of Roman, Italic, Infant, Infant Italic, Garamond, Garamond Italic, Upright Cursive, Small Caps, and Unicase and 5 weights which are Light, Regular, Medium, SemiBold, and Bold. Cormorant was created in its entirety by Christian Thalmann. Christian did not use any specific designs of any other prior text for the design of this font.
